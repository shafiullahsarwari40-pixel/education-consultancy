FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common student questions about studying in Turkey.

Do I need YÖS?

Most programs do not require YÖS for international students. We help you identify the right universities and entry paths.

Can I apply with only a diploma?

Yes, you can apply with a high school diploma and transcript. We also support document evaluation and authentication.

How long does admission take?

Admission timing depends on the program and university, but most applications are reviewed within 4–8 weeks.

Can I study in English?

Many Turkish universities offer English-taught programs. We help you find the best fit and prepare your application.

Do you help with visas?

Yes, we provide visa guidance, document preparation, and support throughout the residence permit process.
